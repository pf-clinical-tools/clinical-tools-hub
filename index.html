Clinical Decision Support Tools

These tools are designed to support clinical decision-making and promote consistent, safe practice. They do not replace clinical judgement, local policy, or specialist advice. Use within appropriate clinical governance frameworks.

1. Anticoagulation Risk Assessment Tool in NVAF

Assists clinicians in assessing stroke risk and anticoagulation considerations in patients with non-valvular atrial fibrillation.

Open Anticoagulation Risk Assessment Tool (NVAF)

2. Clinical Interpretation of Diastolic Function in Suspected Heart Failure

Clinician-led interpretative framework for suspected heart failure, emphasising probability, confidence, and clinical context rather than rigid diastolic grading.

Open Clinical Interpretation of Diastolic Function Tool

3. Peri-operative Catheterisation and TWOC Decision Aid

Decision support for safe urinary catheterisation and TWOC in femoral and pelvic fracture patients.

Open Catheterisation & TWOC Decision Aid

4. Peri-operative PE / DVT Management Decision Support Tool

Supports multidisciplinary decision-making in femoral fracture patients with acute PE or DVT.

Open Peri-operative PE / DVT Decision Support Tool
